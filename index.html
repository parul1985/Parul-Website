About me

I am a PhD candidate at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Rutgers University. I am a member of the Cyber-Physical Systems Lab directed by Dr. Dario Pompili. My research interests span mobile computing, distributed computing, and wireless networks. My thesis work focuses on harnessing the paradigm of approximate computing to enable applications in resource-limited environments. I was previously a graduate student at the University of Utah and worked with Dr. Adam Bolton.
